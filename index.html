About

I would like to greet you, I am Kes ∴ Sol Kai , a visual artist. I have been shaping my artistic vision since 2023. Indeed, art has been my inseparable companion since the age when I became self-aware. At this stage, I am drawn to painting, illustration, 3D art, graphic design and video etudes. In my works, I explore themes of identity, depicting what is often invisible - stories emerging from or trapped within the body. I also run my online poster shop and a small brand, Code 919, which will soon be publicly revealed.
